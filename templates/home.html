AI-Powered Medicine & Prescription Verifier
for Senior Citizens and Visually Impaired Users

Designed for Senior Citizens, Visually Impaired Users, Caregivers, and Home Users
A pre-consumption safety verification tool that checks whether the medicine matches the prescription before use.
Main Use Case: After receiving or buying medicine, and before consuming it, the user can compare the medicine strip/box with the doctor’s prescription to verify medicine name, expiry date, dosage instructions, duration, and prescription match status.
How to Use:
1. Upload medicine and prescription images manually, or use voice-guided camera capture.
2. Click Read Instructions to hear spoken guidance.
3. Click Start Voice Commands to begin guided flow.
4. The system can ask to capture medicine first, then prescription.
5. After both are captured, analysis starts automatically.
6. The result will be shown and automatically spoken aloud.
Voice Commands You Can Say:
“yes”, “ok”, “read instructions”, “open medicine camera”, “open prescription camera”, “capture”, “analyze”, “read result”, “stop”, “clear”, “clear all”
